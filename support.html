Getting started

How do I log an expense?

Tap the + button on the Expenses tab (or the Insight tab) to add a new expense. Enter the amount and a short name, then tap Save.

What is a No-spend day?

A no-spend day is one where you didn't spend anything. Tap No spend today on the Expenses tab to mark it. No-spend days count toward your streak.

What is a streak?

Your streak is the number of consecutive days you've either logged an expense or marked a no-spend day. It helps you build a daily check-in habit.

Reminders & notifications

How do I turn on reminders?

Open Settings → Notifications and enable the reminder you want. The first time, iOS will ask you to allow notifications.

Reminders aren't arriving

Open the iOS Settings app, find Simplit, and confirm notifications are enabled. Also check that Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode isn't silencing them.

Can I change the reminder time?

Yes. In Simplit Settings → Notifications, tap Reminder time and pick a time.

Sync & data

How does iCloud sync work?

If you're signed in to iCloud, Simplit syncs your data privately through your iCloud account. We never see your data — sync happens directly between your devices through Apple.

What if iCloud isn't available?

Simplit works fully offline. Your data stays on the device. You'll see a status in Settings → Account if iCloud isn't connected.

How do I export my data?

Export is on our roadmap. In the meantime, contact support if you need help retrieving your data.

Premium

What does Premium include?

Premium unlocks full expense history, unlimited tracking, and additional assistant features. See Settings → Account in the app for the current plans.

How do I restore a purchase?

Go to Settings → Account → Premium and tap Manage. iOS handles billing and restores through your Apple ID.

How do I cancel?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the iOS Settings app, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and choose Simplit.
