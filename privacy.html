Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

This policy explains what Simplit ("we", "us", "the app") collects, where your data lives, and the choices you have. It applies to the Simplit iOS app published by Saloni Khobragade.

The short version: your expense data stays on your device and in your private iCloud. We don't collect it, sell it, or send it to advertisers.

What we store

  • Expenses, reminders, recurring items, and notes you enter into the app.
  • App settings such as appearance, notification preferences, and reminder time.
  • Streak and check-in history derived from your activity.

All of this is stored on your device using Apple's local database. If you're signed in to iCloud, it's also mirrored to your private iCloud container so it syncs across your devices.

Where your data lives

On your device

The primary copy lives on your iPhone or iPad in app-private storage. Uninstalling the app removes the local copy.

In your iCloud (optional)

If iCloud is available and enabled on your device, your data syncs through your private CloudKit container. We — the developers — cannot see or access this data. Apple's privacy terms apply.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device. The contents of a reminder do not leave your device.

Assistant features (Let's Talk)

If you use Simplit's assistant features (sometimes called "Let's Talk"), the prompts you send may be transmitted to our hosted AI service to generate a reply. We process these messages only to produce a response and to debug failures. We do not use them to train models.

If your build does not have an assistant server configured, assistant features are disabled and no data leaves your device for this purpose.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive purchase receipts so we can unlock premium features on your account. We do not see your payment details.

Diagnostics & analytics

Simplit may collect minimal, anonymous diagnostics (such as crash reports and feature usage counts) to keep the app stable. This is configurable per build and never includes the contents of your expenses or messages.

If you have opted out of "Share with App Developers" in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics, Apple will not share crash logs with us.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not run third-party ad networks inside the app.
  • We do not share your expense data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing partners.

Children

Simplit is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices

  • Disable iCloud sync — turn off iCloud for Simplit in iOS Settings → [Your Apple ID] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud.
  • Disable notifications — iOS Settings → Simplit → Notifications.
  • Delete your data — uninstalling Simplit removes the local copy. To also remove the iCloud copy, sign in to iCloud.com or use iOS Settings → iCloud → Manage Storage.
  • Data request — email us at salonikhobragade@icloud.com for any data subject request (access, correction, deletion).

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we'll update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, surface a notice in the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email salonikhobragade@icloud.com.
